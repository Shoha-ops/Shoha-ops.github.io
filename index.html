My favorite movies

I'd like to tell about some of my favorite movies. I mean, not movies, I prefer anime, so I'll tell about my favorite animes.

First anime is Shuumatsu no Valkyrie: Record of Ragnarok. In this anime, every 1000 years, Gods of every pantheon hold a conference to decide, whether or no let humanity continue living. With recent conference, gods decided to destroy the whole mankind, but at the very last second, one of the eldest Valkyries, whose name is Brunhilda, made the proposition to let humanity live by letting them go against the gods in one-on-one fights. Thirteen gods will fight against thirteen human champions. By the way, the "HAO!" that I mentioned before is the phrase of one of the humanity fighters(he is on my pfp), Qin Shihuang Di, it means "Good!" Here is the link for IMDB.

Another favorite of mine is JoJo's Bizarre Adventure. This anime follows the multi-generational saga of the Joestar family, whose members are fated to battle supernatural foes. The story is split into different parts, each with a new protagonist and setting, starting with Jonathan Joestar in 19th-century England who must master the power of Hamon to defeat his vampiric adopted brother, Dio Brando. Later generations wield incredible psychic powers known as Stands, continuing the fight against Dio and his malevolent influence on the world. Here is the link for IMDB.

The last anime and the most favorite of mine is "To your eternity", I won't lie if I say that this anime is masterpiece, every frame of it. It has pretty simple plot but such a strong execution. It started with a sphere, but not just sphere, sphere that can take looks of those who feels emotional pain, like dying. It can absorb it look and transform into it. At the very beginning it had no meaning in life, no feelings, just an object. So, during its path, it obtained human-factor, such feelings like pain, happiness and others, every second of it makes feel sooooo much different feelings, it can not be described using words. Here is the link for IMDB... HAO!
